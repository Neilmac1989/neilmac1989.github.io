Variety of options

The Yellow Mustard Seed comes from the idea that small things can grow into something meaningful over time. The yellow mustard seed may be tiny, but it develops into something strong, and that idea really stuck. Based in Alberta, this site is about learning as you go and trying new ideas. One of the most obvious uses of mustard seed is, of course, mustard, but it’s also great for adding flavour to soups, sauces, pickles, and spice mixes. They’re small, but they pack a punch. It’s also a symbol of growth, showing how small beginnings can still make a big impact.

Who we are

I created The Yellow Mustard Seed as a student project for my Web Business Essentials course, based in Edmonton, Alberta. I’ve been developing websites over the past year and a half, using it to learn how to plan, design, and build a site from the ground up. Alongside this, I bring 18 years of experience using spices in everyday cooking, especially mustard seed, which influenced the flavour-focused theme and practical approach of the content. Looking ahead, I plan to keep improving the website as my skills grow. The value of this project is combining real-world spice knowledge with hands-on web learning.
